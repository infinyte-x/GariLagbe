Our Story

Not long ago, in Bangladesh in the early 2023s, As a college project we thought of this idea. An idea about making Car Rental Service to the next level In our country. Just an idea can be very powerful, as they can make history. And GariLagbe? Rent-A-Car was born.

Our Mission

Our mission was and still is that, How we can make Renting Car with just our Smartphone in a minute. Our goal is to provide car renting service to everyone in Bangladesh in a very simple process. Provide better service than other service providers and to provide affordable and accessible services.
